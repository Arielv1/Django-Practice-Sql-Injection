Welcome to PAI - Protection Against Injection

This website is all about SQL Injections - what they are, how to perform them, ways to detect and methods to mitigate them.
You'll be able to Learn about 3 main types of SQL Injections - InBand OutBand and Blind SQL injections
After registering and creating an account you'll be able to practice your knowledge about SQL and SQL injections by solving our problems with ask of your to perform SQL injections in a controlled environment.
We believe that the best way to learn about SQL injections and how to defend against them in particular is to actually perform the attack yourself - by doing so you gain knowledge about how the attack actually works which makes it easier to know to defend against it.




This is the thesis and final project of Michael Hamami and Ariel Vainshtien, Software Engineering degree students of Afeka College of Engineering
